Validators will be marked for suspension if their node is inactive for a longer period of time. To prevent suspension ensure your node is up-to-date and active. Once your node is active you will no longer be primed for suspension.

Once it has been inactive for a longer period of time the validation will be suspended.

  • Suspended validators don’t earn rewards
  • Your delegators will stop earning rewards
  • Your delegators will be notified about the suspension

To lift the suspension and earn rewards again ensure your node is up-to-date and click “Resume validation” in the “Change validating status” menu.
